Budget optimization

Zemanta One will optimize your campaign budget across ad groups in order to hit your campaign goal. Budget will be paced evenly throughout the flight time of the campaign.
If you want to run on all available media sources, each ad group requires minimum $30 (or equivalent in your currency) daily spend cap. Make sure your campaign has enough budget to be paced evenly throughout the flight time or use less media sources to prevent your campaign to run out of budget before the end date.
